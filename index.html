Informations

Hey ! I'm Ian Busolo, a SaaS designer based in Nairobi-Kenya, creating digital products for the past. I like to keep things simple - regular updates, clear deadlines, and no confusion about what's happening. You'll always know what's happening with your project.

Frequently Asked Questions
  • +
    Do you only work with SaaS companies ?
    I work with a diverse range of clients - from agencies and startups to entrepreneurs across various industries
  • +
    Can you help with an existing product redesign ?
    I can help redesign your existing product to improve its user experience or align it with a new branding.
  • +
    Can we hire you on a retainer basis ?
    Yes, I offer retainer agreements that ensure dedicated availability and consistent support for your design needs.
  • +
    What can you do aside product and website?
    I can help with branding, marketing assets, social visuals, and pitch deck creation - everything digital.
  • +
    Only SaaS or Mobile app also ?
    I don't work on native mobile apps, but I can help clients whose SaaS products include a mobile version.
