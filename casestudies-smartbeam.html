Case studies / Smartbeam

Improving experience for budget application for construction team

Introduction

Smarteam is a budget management app for construction team. I've worked with Smartbeam on improving and simplifying their product.

Scope of work
  • Product Design
  • SAAS
  • UX
Year

2021

UX

Understanding the context & userflow

Smartbeam empowers collaborators in a construction project to centralize information, progress, and data. It enables construction managers to monitor the building's development and allows promoters to oversee costs and deadlines effectively.

Given the diverse user personas within the app, conducting research and creating documents to clarify the context and objectives were necessary for complex features.

Design

Optimizing space & giving clarity

My aim was to expand the product with new features while maintaining uniformity in the user interface and experience. Given the app's requirement to present extensive information, we focused on optimizing every element to accommodate all the necessary data effectively.

Mobile app

Mobile for a different application

The mobile application was primarily designed for on-site workers, serving as a useful tool to assist them in monitoring and tracking the day-to-day progress and developments on the construction site.
